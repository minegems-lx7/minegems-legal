Privacy Policy for MineGems

Last updated: April 21, 2026

MineGems ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the app, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

MineGems does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personal contact information.

The app stores gameplay data (progress, lives, purchases) locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to us.

2. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers (IDFA/IDFV), ad interaction data, and diagnostic information to deliver and measure ads. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Apple App Store — for processing in-app purchases. Purchase data is handled by Apple under its own privacy policy.

3. Advertising and Tracking

The app displays rewarded advertisements provided by Google AdMob. With your permission (via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt), your device's advertising identifier may be used for personalized advertising. You can revoke this permission at any time in your device's Settings under Privacy > Tracking.

4. In-App Purchases

MineGems offers optional in-app purchases for virtual items (extra lives, boosters). All payments are processed by Apple via the App Store. We do not receive or store your payment information.

5. Children's Privacy

MineGems is rated suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through third-party services used by the app, please contact us.

6. Data Retention

Local gameplay data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear its data. Data collected by third parties is retained according to their respective policies.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the EU, CCPA in California), you may have rights regarding data collected by third parties. Please contact the third-party services listed above to exercise these rights regarding data they collect.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
